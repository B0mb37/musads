About Us

The Maseno University Spoken Arts & Debate Society, under the Maseno University School of Law is a group of passionate advocates for the art of open discourse and the unwavering support of free speech. Founded on the principles of intellectual engagement and critical thinking, our society is a vibrant community of individuals who believe in the power of words to shape the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear: to provide a platform where diverse ideas and perspectives can be expressed freely and respectfully. We firmly believe that open dialogue and the exhange of ideas are the cornerstones of personal and societal growth.

What We Do

Through a variety of events, including debates, public speaking workshops and discussion forums, we encourage our members to challenge themselves, broaden their horizons, and develop their communication skills. We aim to create a safe and inclusive environment where everyone can freely express their thoughts and opinions.
